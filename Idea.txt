Application for storing information about employees. Allow to create employee card with number of properties.
	
Candidate properties:
1. Name Surname *required
2. Department
2. Title *required + Level (junior, middle, senior - choose from the list)
4. Rate 
5. Linkedin page 
6. Primary skills (tags) *Required 
7. Years of experience (number). Enter date of career start, program count years + months *required
8. Comment (+Author, +Date) 
9. Attached files (Resume) *required
10. Personal site, portfolio on behance (url)
11. Contact information: skype, mail *required
