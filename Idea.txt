Application for storing information about employees. Allow to create employee card with number of properties.
	
Candidate properties:
1. Name Surname *required
2. Department
3. Title *required + Level (junior, middle, senior - choose from the list) 
4. Linkedin page 
5. Primary skills (tags) *Required 
6. Years of experience (number). Enter date of career start, program count years + months *required
7. Comment (+Author, +Date) 
8. Attached files (Resume) *required
9. Personal site, portfolio on behance (url)
10. Contact information: skype, mail *required
